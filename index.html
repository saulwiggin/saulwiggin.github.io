Icarus

Transformation optics holds real potential to revolutionise the optics and antennas industry. Saul Wiggin's Icarus Blog contains up to date information of Saul Wiggin. Work regarding all-dieletric optical systems using transformation optics. This is based on the technology on metamaterials and it is possible to design all sorts of interesting things such as:

  • Invisibility cloaks
  • Flat Lenses
  • Sub-wavelength Imagers
