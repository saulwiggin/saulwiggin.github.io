My Music

I have always enjoyed music. I played the saxophone since I was 14 years old. I founded my school's first jazz/funk band. This was part of a jazz funk combo I performed in with two other class mates Lawerence Panter on Keys and Henry Pollock on Bass Guitar. We Would practice at the house of Specials bassist Sir Horace gentleman. I also performed with a ska band rude boys don't cry. We recorded a record and performed ska gigs across rugby and Coventry. The gigs with both jazz and ska bands were paid. I also performed with Warwick Universities own Big Band. We performed at a jazz club on tour in Brighton.
